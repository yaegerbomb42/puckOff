Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use puckOFF, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you sign in via Google or email/password authentication through Firebase.
  • Game Data: Match statistics, inventory, progression, and in-game purchases stored in Firebase.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about game sessions, feature usage, and performance metrics.
  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution, and operating system for compatibility and performance optimization.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To save your progress, inventory, and match history
  • To process in-game purchases via Stripe
  • To improve gameplay and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Advertising. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. Google Ads Privacy
  • Stripe: Payment processing for Zoin purchases. Stripe Privacy Policy

Cookies and Advertising

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Data Security

We use Firebase's built-in security features and Firestore Security Rules to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

puckOFF is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. You can also clear your local data at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at the game's support channels.
